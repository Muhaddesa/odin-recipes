Qabli Palaw

TAushak is an Afghan dish made of pasta dumplings filled with chives, with a tomato sauce, topped with yogurt and dried mint. A time-consuming meal to prepare, it is usually served on holidays or at special gatherings. It is similar to mantu, which is also popular in Afghanistan.

Ingredients

  • Lamb
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • yogurt
  • Raisins
  • Rice

Steps:

  1. Prepare rice and Meat
  2. cook carrots and onions
  3. Layer the rice and Meat
  4. Serve Qabli Palaw
