Ashak

Ashak is an Afghan dish made of pasta dumplings filled with chives, with a tomato sauce, topped with yogurt and dried mint. A time-consuming meal to prepare, it is usually served on holidays or at special gatherings. It is similar to mantu, which is also popular in Afghanistan.

Ingredients

  • chives
  • Leeks
  • Onions
  • yogurt
  • tomato
  • sauce

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dumpling filling
  2. Assemble the dumplings
  3. Cook the dumplings
  4. Prepare the sauce and serve
